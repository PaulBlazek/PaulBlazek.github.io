A brief introduction to The Blazek Gazette

General

How you can add your own article and general structure


The Blazek Gazette is an online place for all sorts of fun stuff! Comics, articles, and other resources are coming soon as new items are added on the site. Send ideas for an article you would like to add to Paul Blazek and he will consider adding them to the site. Anything of general interest can be added. The main things I am looking for are creative postings, such as board games you have invented, lego creations, writing projects, and comics. With any luck there will soon be a full page with numerous contributations from many of our local Blazeks.
